Travel Itinerary

Here is a detailed itinerary for the 6 Days and 5 Nights (Honeymoon Trip)

Day 1 (Arrival + Carboyns Cove Beach + Cellular Jail + Light and sound Visit)

The Day will start with the Arrival of the Guest to Port Blair. Our Representatives will receive the Guest in the Airport, assist them with Hotel Transfers and Brief them about the Days Program. Based on the time available after the Check-in and Lunch. First we will take tour of Carboyns Cove Beach and then visit the Cellular Jail on a guided tour and finally witness the Light and Sound Show.


Day 2 (Departure to Havelock (Morning Cruise) + Radha Nagar Beach)

Today will start a little early in the morning, in order to the catch the first available Cruise to Havelock island. As the guest arrive to Havelock our escorts will receive them there and assist them with hotel/ Resort transfers. The plan for the day will be to visit the famous Radha Nagar Beach and only return back to the hotel after the Sun set which is very beautiful to witness from the Beach.


Day 3 (Continue in Havelock + KalaPathar Beach Excursion)

During the day we will visit a small beach on the other side of Havelock island , this beach is pretty small but good for swimming and very beautiful.


Day 4 (Departure to Neil island from Havelock + Sightseeing in Neil island)

Today after the Breakfast we will take the first Cruise to Neil island. After arrival check-in to the hotel in Neil island, we will break for couple of hours for Lunch, and as per our Schedule of stay in the Island in the coming days, split our sightseeing Visit. During our stay in the Island we will Visit the LaxmanPur Beach, BharatPur Beach, Natural Bridge.


Day 5 (Return from Neil island and visit Ross island)

Return from Neil island and visit Ross island.


Day 6 (Departure drop to Airport)

Drop you to the Airport.
